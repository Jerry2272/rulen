About Rulen

Rulen - Africa's rural and semi-urban focused learn to earn digital skills talent development platform has a mission to empower smart talents with in-demand skills and access to high quality jobs upon completion of our programme. We offer a dynamic range of highly relevant digital skills courses, delivered by experienced facilitators with industry leaders serving as mentors.

The demand for digital skills is on an all time high globally as the world has moved to tech. Africa, home to over 70% of the world youth population is struggling with unemployment. While the opportunities to access these skills exist in urban areas, semi-urban and rural areas have difficulties accessing digital skills; as a result, interested learners have to either relocate, deal with problem of logistics, competition and/or give into crime. Even though the consciousness of digital skills investment is growing, there is a wide gap as a result of lack of infrastructure, technical know-how and resources.
